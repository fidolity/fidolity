# Staking Guide

## What is Staking?

Staking allows you to lock your FDLT tokens to:
- Earn passive rewards from platform fees
- Access premium features
- Participate in governance
- Support network security

## Current Rates

- **Base APY**: 26.18%
- **Boosted APY**: 45.42% (with campaign)
- **Total Value Locked**: 59.4M FDLT

## How to Stake

### Step 1: Navigate to Staking Page

1. Go to [fidolity.com/staking](https://fidolity.com/staking)
2. Connect your Solana wallet

### Step 2: Check Your Balance

Your available FDLT balance will be displayed at the top.

### Step 3: Enter Stake Amount

1. Enter the amount of FDLT to stake
2. Or use quick percentage buttons:
   - 25%
   - 50%
   - 75%
   - 100%

### Step 4: Confirm Transaction

1. Review the transaction details:
   - Stake amount
   - Current APY
   - Estimated rewards
2. Click **"Stake"**
3. Approve in your wallet
4. Wait for confirmation

## Rewards

### How Rewards Work

- Rewards are calculated daily
- Distributed from platform fees
- Auto-compounded
- Claimable anytime

### Claiming Rewards

1. Go to Staking page
2. View **"Rewards Earned"**
3. Click **"Claim Rewards"**
4. Approve transaction

## Unstaking

### How to Unstake

1. Go to Staking page
2. Click **"Unstake"** tab
3. Enter amount to unstake
4. Confirm transaction

### Important Notes

- No lock-up period currently
- Unstaking stops reward accrual
- Can unstake anytime

## FAQ

### What is APY?

Annual Percentage Yield - the yearly return on your staked tokens.

### When do I receive rewards?

Rewards accrue daily and can be claimed anytime.

### Is there a minimum stake?

No minimum required, but consider gas fees.

### Can I add more to my stake?

Yes! Just stake additional tokens.

### What happens if I unstake?

You'll stop earning rewards but can restake anytime.

## Risks

- Impermanent loss (not applicable here)
